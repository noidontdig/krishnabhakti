Vegetarian Cooking Classes:
now featuring a lot of people

First, the vegetarian cooking class is in the Kimmel Center, room 805 at 7:00pm every Monday night. If you join the e-mail list, you will get reminders of the classes, and also be informed if they are cancelled.

What should I bring to the class? Defintely not pants. In the spirit of sustainability, we encourage everyone to bring their own forks and reusable plates/Tupperware, but disposable versions of both of those are provided. Many people also write the recipes down, so if you want to do that, add a pencil and some paper to your list of stuff to bring. Also, an open mind and a smile.

The class is in the form of a demo - We usually go through all the steps of making a simple vegetarian dish that takes 10-20 minutes to prepare and cook. While the food is cooking, there is usually a short presentation on one positive aspect of vegetarianism. After the food is done, we all eat together while listening to traditional Indian music and have a good time.
